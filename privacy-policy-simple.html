Privacy Policy for Math Quest

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Math Quest is a free educational math application for children. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Math Quest does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

What This App Does NOT Do

Math Quest:

  • Does NOT require internet connection
  • Does NOT collect personal information
  • Does NOT share data with third parties
  • Does NOT contain advertisements
  • Does NOT track user behavior
  • Does NOT use analytics services
  • Is completely FREE with no in-app purchases

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Player name (chosen by the user)
  • Selected grade level (0-5)
  • Game statistics (questions answered, accuracy, points)
  • Selected avatar and language preferences

Your Data Stays On Your Device

All data stored by Math Quest:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Can be completely deleted by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Math Quest is designed for children and complies with applicable children's privacy laws including COPPA and GDPR. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. There are no external dependencies that could access your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
